How the Lab is Different

The old way of learning bachata sensual does not work. You spend most of your time staring at instructors and trying to imitate their movement. Our new and innovative approach will help you level up faster.


You will be actively learning and actively teaching others the simple steps that you learn. This will enable you to use your muscle memory and exercise cognitive repetitions that can help you pick up the dance faster and feel the movements rather than just watching a movement without a good idea of how it should feel.


This approach will maximize your practice time and help you understand the feeling of the music better.
