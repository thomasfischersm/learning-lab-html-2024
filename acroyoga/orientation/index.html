Online Orientation

Every student has to go through the online orientation before attenting their first in-person class. To ensure that students actually went through the orientation, there is a quiz at the end. You'll receive an access code that allows you to sign up on Eventbrite.

Why?

  • Know the safety rules and how things work so your first class isn’t overwhelming.
  • Skip repeating the same orientation when new students join.
  • Arrive ready to jump straight into physical practice.

Quiz

Relax — the quiz is open book. It only covers what you need to know in class. For example, if someone calls “Down!”, you need to know exactly what to do. You won’t be asked about background or trivia.

Because it only covers essential knowledge, the passing score is 100%.

Take this seriously. If you watch the videos and pay attention, this will be a slam dunk. If you try to wing it, you risk creating safety issues and disrupting the class.
